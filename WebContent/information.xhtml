Content

Tabloid Proteome is a database of protein association network generated using publically available mass spectrometry based experiments in PRIDE. These associations includes; protein-protein interaction, complex formation, or different role in single pathway. All experiments in PRIDE are re-processed using re-spin pipeline to determine protein present in each experiment with their peptide counts in each experiment. Peptide counts for each proteins are used to calculate the highly co-occurring proteins in various mass spectrometry based experiments. Tabloid Proteome is the collection of these co-occurring protein pairs, together with their existing biological relation in existing knowledgebases. The biological relations included in Tabloid Proteome are pathways from Reactome, protein-protein interaction from IntAct and BioGRID, protein complex from CORUM, paralog information from Ensembl, and diseases from DisGeNet. Tabloid Proteome also include mapping of proteins with GO biological process, molecular function and cellular component to suggest possible biological relation for the proteins with no biological relation in existing knowledgebases.
