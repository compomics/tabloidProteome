Content

ProtANet is protein association network generated using publically available mass spectrometry based experiments in PRIDE. All experiments in PRIDE are re-processed using re-spin pipeline to determine protein present in each experiment with their peptide counts in each experiment. Peptide counts for each proteins are used to calculate the highly co-occurring proteins in various mass spectrometry based experiments. ProtANet is the collection of these co-occurring protein pairs, together with their existing biological relation in existing four knowledgebases. The biological relations included in ProtANet are pathways from Reactome, protein-protein interaction from IntAct, protein complex from CORUM, and paralog information from Ensembl. ProtANet also include mapping of proteins with GO biological process, molecular function and cellular component to suggest possible biological relation for the proteins with no existing relation in existing knowledgebases.
